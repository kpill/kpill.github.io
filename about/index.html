ABOUT THE WEBSITE

I originally built this website as a way to teach myself web design with HTML and CSS. On that note, please let me know if you have any suggestions related to the layout of the site. I appreciate the feedback!

The blog is my creative outlet. I'm big into writing and journaling, and many of the posts on the website have been adapted from entries in my personal journal. Everyday I have the opportunity to learn something from another person. My hope is that I can give back by sharing my own revelations and lessons in the blog, and continue the chain of inspiration.

ABOUT KAMALA

I moved around a bit as a kid, but I did most of my growing up in the South Bay Area in California. I earned my Bachelor's degree in Bioengineering from UC Berkeley, with a minor in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. Now I'm doing my Master's in Business Analytics at UCLA Anderson School of Management. So yes, Californian through and through.

As I write this we're in the middle of the coronavirus pandemic. Apart from school and blogging, I've been keeping myself sane with a bunch of different hobbies. Recently it's been a lot of yoga and a lot of guitar. If you're reading this during the pandemic, I hope you're staying safe and being well. If you're not reading this during the pandemic, then I should probably update this page.
